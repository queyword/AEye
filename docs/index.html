Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

AEye does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device with no network connections, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party services.

AEye does not access your camera, microphone, location, contacts, or any other device data.
